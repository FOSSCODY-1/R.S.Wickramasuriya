Instructions

In this game there's a grid of 3 by 3 squares. This is a two player game. Each player is automatically assigned to a symbol(Cross of Nought). Players can fill the empty squares by the assigned symbol one after the other. Each players target is to make a straight line of his/her own symbol. It can be horizontal, vertical or diagonal. The first player to make that straight line will be the winner.


There is two modes in this game. You can either play Multiplayer with a friend or you can play Singleplayer. Enjoy!
